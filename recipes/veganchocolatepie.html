Vegan Chocolate Pie

Ingredients

  • 2 cups almond milk
  • 1 (5 ounce) package non-instant chocolate pudding mix
  • ¼ cup vegan chocolate chips
  • 1 vegan graham cracker crust
  • ½ cup vegan whipped cream (Optional)

Steps

  1. Combine almond milk and pudding mix in a saucepan and bring to a boil, stirring constantly, over medium heat.
  2. As soon as pudding boils, reduce heat to low and continue cooking and stirring until pudding thickens, about 5 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in chocolate chips until melted.
  4. Pour into graham crust and refrigerate until set, about 2 hours.
  5. Top with vegan whipped cream.
